## 🏗️ Required Sections

Your index.html must utilize semantic HTML tags. You can use `flexbox` or `grid` the way you want. The page should be divided into the following blocks:

1. A Header: Contains the site title "Internet Tools" and a navigation menu.
1. Hero Section: A full-width banner with a background image overlay and a welcome message: "Welcome to Internet Tools - COSC 2006".
1. About Section: An `<article>` containing a heading and at least two paragraphs.
2. Cards: A responsive grid displaying three cards:
      1. 2 Image Cards: Featuring an image, title, and description.
      2. 1 Info Card: A text-only card with a top-border accent and a "More Tips" button.
3. Contact Form: A section containing a form with Name, Email, Interest (Dropdown), and Message fields.
4. Footer: Copyright information and social links.

!!!info "Note on links"
    You can keep all links point to the same page `href="#"`
